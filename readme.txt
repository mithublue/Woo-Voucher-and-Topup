Now let your customer purchase voucher from your store, refill/topup their their account and use that fund to purchase product.

== Description ==

DO you need to produce voucher for your WooCommerce store and let your customer purchase it so that they can puchase anything with that voucher later ?
Or, do you want your customer to topup their account with fund and use that fund to purchase anything later ?

These are exactly what this plugin does.

== Demo ==

You can try the full featured demo of this plugin <a href="http://cybercraftit.com/demo/testwp/voucher-topup/demo/">here</a>.
There, in admin panel, you can find settings in WooCommerce > Settings > Voucher and Topup. Also you can create voucher product from Products menu.

= Features =

* You can create voucher for your store
* You can give discount to product purchased by voucher (Pro)
* Discount type can be both fixed or percentage (Pro)
* You can set discount amount (Pro)
* You can enable customer to topup their account by any amount they wish (Pro)
* You can set the minium and maxium value for topup (Pro)
* Quick support by our official forum (Pro)
* Limited things get the unlimited ;) (Pro)
* Regular pro update (Pro)
